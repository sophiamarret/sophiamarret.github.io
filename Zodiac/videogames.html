• In the 2007 video game Manhunt 2, the player character Daniel Lamb is modeled after the Zodiac Killer's composite sketch.
  • The main antagonist of 2010's Cause of Death volume 4, "Zero", is largely inspired by the Zodiac Killer.
  • Watch Dogs 2 features a pre-order mission where the protagonist Marcus Holloway has to find the Zodiac Killer and solve his mystery.
  • Zodiac is a passive item added in the Binding of Isaac: Afterbirth expansion, represented by the crosshair symbol.
  • In the 2017 game Serial Cleaner, the level "Horoscope Date" has the player character hired by copycat killer to clean up a crime scene similar to the Lake Berryessa murder. The player character takes home the copycat's hood as a memento.
  • In the upcoming 2020 video game This is the Zodiac Speaking, Players will play the role of journalist Robert Hartnell, a fictional character based on Bryan Hartnell, who survived eight stab wounds to the back from the Zodiac Killer.[12]
